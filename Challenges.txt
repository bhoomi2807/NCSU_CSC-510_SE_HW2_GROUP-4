## Julia

a. Not object oriented.
b. Mainly built for numerical computaion.
c. Add on features are not maintained well.
d. Does not integrate well with other languages.


## Ruby

a. Slow processing as compared to other languages.
b. Hard to find good documentation.
c. Not scalable.
d. Upgrades get released after ages making it outdated quite often.

## Scala

a. Limited developer pool.
b. Hard to understand due to hybrid nature of being object-oriented and functional.
c. Limited backward compatibility.
d. Poor IDE support.
e. Lack of documentation.
f. Creation of list of list is not possible.
g. Difficulties in capturing input from user.
